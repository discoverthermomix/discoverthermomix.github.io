The world's smallest smartest kitchen

With over 20 built-in functions, it lets you prepare wholesome meals from scratch in just a few simple steps. From chopping and blending to steaming and slow cooking, Thermomix® ensures your meals are made with fresh ingredients and no preservatives. Ideal for both home cooks and professionals, the TM6 saves time, reduces energy consumption, and helps you create healthy, delicious meals effortlessly.
