The world's smallest smartest kitchen

Thermomix® TM7 is your all-in-one kitchen assistant that makes healthy cooking easier and faster. With over 20 built-in functions, it lets you prepare wholesome meals from scratch in just a few simple steps. From chopping and blending to steaming and slow cooking, Thermomix® ensures your meals are made with fresh ingredients and no preservatives. Ideal for both home cooks and professionals, the TM7 saves time, reduces energy consumption, and helps you create healthy, delicious meals effortlessly.

Save time and money

Cooking with Thermomix® is not only quick, but also economical. By preparing homemade meals, you reduce the need for expensive takeout or pre-made food. Its versatility helps you save time and money, while still enjoying high-quality, nutritious dishes.

Ease of use is at the heart of Thermomix®. The guided cooking function walks you through each recipe, making even complex dishes accessible to all skill levels. This means you can enjoy stress-free cooking and spend more time savoring your meals.

Crafted to last

Engineered by Vorwerk, a German company with over 140 years of history, the TM7 is crafted to last. Its precision technology includes a scale accurate to the gram, perfect for bakers, and multiple cleaning modes to make clean-up easier than ever.

Plus, the TM7 keeps evolving with software updates that bring new features and improvements right to your kitchen. Thermomix® TM7 is more than a kitchen gadget — it's an investment in time-saving, healthy cooking, with the flexibility to cater to any culinary style.

Creative and economical

Thermomix® also encourages creativity in the kitchen. With access to thousands of recipes through its Cookidoo® platform, you can explore different cuisines and try new flavors. Whether you want to master a classic dish or experiment with something new, Thermomix® opens up endless culinary possibilities.

Thermomix® supports a sustainable lifestyle. Its energy-efficient design and the ability to cook from scratch means you can reduce food waste, lower your grocery bills, and make meals that are good for both you and the environment.
